STEP 1: CREATE A GOOGLE DRIVE FOLDER
Go to https://drive.google.com and sign in with your email. Click the “+ New” button on the left, then
select “Folder.” Name the folder using this format:
Firstname_HighlightVideoClips
 (Example: Kwame_HighlightVideoClips). This folder will be the main place where all your clips, images,
and documents are kept together and organized.
STEP 2: UPLOAD ALL YOUR FILES INSIDE THE FOLDER
Inside your new folder, upload all the files we’ve asked you to prepare:
10–20 game clips (named like: clip_1_dribble.mp4, clip_2_pass.mp4, etc.)
6 images (game photos, etc.)
Completed CV (Word or PDF version)
Make sure all videos are clear, steady, and labeled properly so we know what skill is being shown in
each. Avoid clutter or random files—keep it clean and organized.
STEP 3: SHARE THE FOLDER WITH US
Once everything is inside the folder, right-click on
the folder and choose “Share.” Then click “Copy
link” and under sharing settings, select:
“Anyone with the link can view.”
 Send that link to us via email or in the CKtraining
platform. This makes your full file set instantly
ready for us to build your CV and highlight video.
